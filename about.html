About Green Kerala

Green Kerala is a pioneering waste management initiative dedicated to transforming waste collection and environmental sustainability in Kerala. Founded with a mission to create cleaner, greener communities, we are committed to innovative and eco-friendly waste management solutions.

Our team comprises environmental experts, local community leaders, and passionate sustainability advocates who believe in the power of responsible waste disposal. We leverage cutting-edge technology and community engagement to revolutionize how waste is collected, sorted, and recycled.

We are more than just a waste collection service. We are a movement towards environmental consciousness, working tirelessly to reduce landfill waste, promote recycling, and create a sustainable future for Kerala's beautiful landscape.
